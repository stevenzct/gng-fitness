FAQ

Answers to common questions before you message us

01Do you accept beginners?

Yes. The gym can offer beginner assessment and trainer guidance for first-time members.

02Can members pay through GCash?

The prototype includes payment proof upload as a future feature. Online payment can be added later.

03How does QR check-in work?

Each member gets a unique QR code. Staff scans it to record attendance and membership status.

04Can this become a mobile app?

Yes. The same system can later be converted into a React Native or Flutter mobile app.
